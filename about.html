About Me

Hello, I'm Isha Chhabra! My journey began with a simple fascination for understanding how our memories are shaped by the world around us. This has led me down a path of unraveling the hidden neural stories within us.

Outside the lab, I find that the same curiosity that fuels my research also leads me to explore new hiking trails, experiment with a new recipe, or planning my next adventure abroad! I enjoy finding a new perspective, whether it's through a competitive game of pickleball or my community work with organizations like S.M.I.L.E. and the United Nations Foundation. These passions for exploration and service are what drive me to make a meaningful impact every day!
