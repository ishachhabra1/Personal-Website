Welcome

Welcome to my professional website. I am currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Oregon studying the neural mechanisms underlying memory and perception. My work integrates a rich fMRI Multimodal Memory Dataset to investigate how the brain processes and reconstructs memory.

On this site, you will find information about my research, publications, other experiences, and ways to get in touch. Feel free to explore my work and reach out with any questions!
